Short Biography of Hajiya Gambo Sawaba:

  • 1933 February 15 Hajiya Gambo Sawaba was born in Lavun local government, Niger state
  • 1943 was when she had to stop schooling after the death of her father
  • 1950 Sawaba join politics at the age of 17. During that time, Northern Nigeria was dominated by the Northern People's Congress,
  • She was imprisoned 16 times for openly advocating against child marriage, forced and unpaid labour and unfair taxes, and canvassed
    for jobs for women, education for girls and full voting rights.
  • 2001 October 14 Sawaba died at the age of 68
